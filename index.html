How to Play

Welcome to my Javasript quiz, here I have demonstrated my front end knowledge by creating a quiz consisting of 10 general knowledge questions and using HTML, CSS and Javasript to bring it to life. To play, simply click on the "Quiz" tab at the top of the page which will take you to Quiz where all you then need to do it click on the answer you believe is correct and hit the submit button. At the end of the quiz you will be given a score out of 10.
